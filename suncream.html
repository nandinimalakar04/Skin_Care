Description

Protecting your skin from the harmful UV rays and preventing sun damage—is just one of the many benefits of the humble sunscreen. Did you know that if you are to religiously use your SPF, it also helps prevent fine lines & wrinkles? Enter Dr. Sheth’s Ceramide & Vitamin C Sunscreen, with SPF 50 PA+++, specially formulated for sensitive Indian skin that comes with broad-spectrum protection from UV Rays. It not only acts as a shield against pollution but also protects the skin from the damaging blue light. Indian skin tends to have a weak barrier, but thanks to the ceramides in the formulation, it works to restore the barrier. Vitamin C in the formulation helps repair skin damage by preventing micro-pigmentation. Hyaluronic acid moisturizes and gives the sunscreen a nourishing yet non-sticky texture.

FAQ
  • How much sunscreen should I apply?

    Spread 2 fingers’ worth of sunscreen on your face and neck, add a palmful for any other exposed area.

  • When should I apply sunscreen?

    It is advisable to apply sunscreen at least 20 minutes before sun exposure & reapply at least every 2 hours.

  • Is the sunscreen suitable for oily skin?

    The lightweight, non-sticky formulation is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive. Hydrating & strengthening ingredients help protect & repair without being too heavy on the skin.

  • Will the SPF form a white layer on my skin?

    No, the formulation doesn’t leave a white cast on the skin.

  • What is the consistency of the sunscreen?

    The consistency is lotion-like which blends effortlessly into the skin without feeling sticky.
